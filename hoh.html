iflix_Hack: Seize Ctrl

DESCRIPTION

Have you ever looked at the iflixapp and went, "I can do better"? Now you have an awesome opportunityto do just that. Join us at iflix_Hack: Seize Ctrl,a special 24 hour Hackathon in conjunction with the season finale of Mr. RobotSeason 2. Come build exciting new features for the iflixapp, and stand a chance to win awesome cash and prizes! Winning ideas may alsobe implemented on the live version of the app. Now that's something you'd wantto brag about!

The winning teams will be walking awaywith cool cash prizes:

  • 1st Prize - RM 5,000 & Prizes
  • 2nd Prize - RM 3,000 & Prizes
  • 3rd Prize - RM 1,000 & Prizes

Winners will also have the opportunity towork with the iflix technical team on other excitingprojects on their platform in future. This Hackathon will be limited to only 15teams, with 3 members per team. Sign-up now before you miss the spot!

Please take note that a Pre-HackBriefing will be held on the 12th Sept 2016 at the same venue for allregistered participants. At least 1 member of each team must attend this briefing.
